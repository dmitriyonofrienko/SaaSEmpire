About holding

SaasEmpire is a group of companies uniting such business areas as: sms hub, software development for the telecom market, insurance, lead generation and many other interesting things.

Our first project started in 2005, and today we've grown so much that we can call ourselves a proud "holding".

We value our employees and contribute to their development, we value our clients and help them to realize even the most intricate ideas, we try to be ahead of time, not forgetting the devotion to tradition.
