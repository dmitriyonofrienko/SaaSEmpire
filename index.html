About Holding

Since our first project in 2007 we grew up into a huge international holding with large bag of experience in such business areas as sms hub, sms testing tool, marketplace for solar energy and insurance marketplace and lots of other services. We develop our products by using artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) technologies.

Our power is in knowledge, experience and employees, these points help us to be ahead of the time and from an idea create innovative useful products for any market in the world.
